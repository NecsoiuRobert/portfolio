Halloween Tinder - a college party app
During the year one of the most awaited and wanted holidays on the globe is Halloween. People all around the world embrace the spooky atmosphere this day brings and celebrate it with all their heart. Most of the students also like Halloween, and they also like to party. Keeping this in mind LSAC and SISC, two of the most popular student non-profit organisations in Bucharest, decided and worked hard enough to held the biggest student party in the city. The team also came with an intriguing idea to bring people together using technology, so LSAC decided that the party should have a Tinder-like application in order to help students attending to party know each other. My task for this project was to design the application in such manner that would blend the spooky atmosphere of the Halloween night with the modern look of Tinder.

It all started on paper

For this application the job was pretty simple, I only had to look at Tinder and make the design similar, the sketching went pretty fast and I could continue with wireframes and the actual design.

The next step was wireframing

After having a sketch of the project, the next step is always wireframing. Wireframing is an important step because it gives both me and the client an idea of how to project will be like.

Reaching the goal

The hard part of this project was making everything fit with the Halloween, finding the right color scheme, doing the illustration and the icons. The icons should be elements from Halloween but would also guide the user through the application.
