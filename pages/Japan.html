Japan
This is another personal project with the theme of Japan, I took what I think is most important in Japan's culture and put it in two pages. The goal was presenting a bit of Japan's architecture and religion in a different way.

It all started on paper

First of all, before I open any design tool, I draw on paper. I find the UX problems that need to be solved and I find solutions.

The next step was wireframing

After having a sketch of the project, the next step is always wireframing. Wireframing is an important step because it gives both me and the client an idea of how to project will be like.

Reaching the goal

The sketch is done, the wireframe is done, all it is to do now is DESIGN! I start applying colors, adding images and working on actual design of the project.
